## Members Area

Originally running on an outdated Wordpress theme, we noticed a clear trend in the number of paying members who were trying to access the members site from their smartphones and tablets.

My task was to take elements of the existing Wordpress theme and create a brand new theme from scratch that adopted the latest techniques in mobile responsiveness.
